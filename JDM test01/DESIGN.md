# Design System Document
 
This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our design system.
 
## Theme
 
Our design system utilizes a **light color mode**, providing a bright and airy user experience.
 
### Color Palette
 
The color palette is derived from a seed color `#3B82F6` with a `fidelity` variant, establishing a strong and consistent brand presence.
 
*   **Primary Color:** `#111827` - A deep, authoritative color used for primary actions, headings, and key brand elements.
*   **Secondary Color:** `#3B82F6` - A vibrant accent color supporting primary actions and highlighting important information.
*   **Tertiary Color:** (Not explicitly defined, using inferred value from base theme) - No specific tertiary color is currently defined.
*   **Neutral Color:** `#FFFFFF` - A clean, crisp white used for backgrounds, surfaces, and non-chromatic elements.
 
### Typography
 
Our typography system ensures readability and visual hierarchy.
 
*   **Headlines:** `plusJakartaSans` - Used for all major titles and headings, conveying a modern and clear tone.
*   **Body Text:** `plusJakartaSans` - Applied to paragraph text and general content for legibility and consistency.
*   **Labels:** `plusJakartaSans` - Used for form labels, small text, and UI annotations.
 
### Shape
 
Our UI elements feature **moderate roundedness (2)**, offering a balanced and approachable aesthetic that is neither overly sharp nor fully pill-shaped.
 
### Spacing
 
We implement **normal spacing (2)**, providing a comfortable and well-organized layout without feeling too dense or excessively spacious. This promotes clarity and ease of interaction.
